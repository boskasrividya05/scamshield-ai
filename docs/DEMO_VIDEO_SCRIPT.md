# ScamShield AI — Demo Video Script (3–4 minutes)

**Tip:** Record your screen with OBS Studio / Windows Game Bar while
narrating. Keep energy up, speak clearly, and keep the mouse movement slow
and deliberate so viewers can follow along.

---

### 0:00 – 0:20 | Hook + Problem Statement
**(Show: Home page hero section)**

> "Every day, thousands of Indians get a call claiming to be from the CBI or
> police, telling them they're under 'digital arrest' — and many lose their
> life savings out of panic. This is ScamShield AI: an AI-powered platform
> built for the Economic Times AI Hackathon 2.0, Problem Statement PS6 —
> AI for Digital Public Safety."

---

### 0:20 – 0:45 | Registration & Login
**(Show: Register page → fill form → redirect to Login → log in)**

> "Getting started takes seconds. I'll register a new account... and log in.
> ScamShield AI uses secure password hashing and session-based
> authentication to keep every user's data safe."

---

### 0:45 – 1:45 | Core AI Fraud Detector (the centerpiece)
**(Show: Detect page → paste a real fraud SMS example → click Analyze)**

> "Here's the heart of the platform — the AI Fraud Detector. I'll paste a
> real-style scam SMS: 'URGENT: Your account will be blocked in 24 hours...'
> and click Analyze."

**(Show: Result appears — FRAUD badge, confidence %, reasons list, tips list)**

> "In under a second, our TF-IDF + Logistic Regression model classifies this
> as FRAUD with 98% confidence. But we don't stop at a label — notice the
> 'Why this verdict' section explains exactly which red-flag keywords were
> detected, and the 'What should you do' section gives concrete prevention
> steps. This transparency is what makes the AI trustworthy, not a black box."

**(Show: Switch tab to URL, paste a phishing URL, analyze)**

> "The same detector also checks website URLs — flagging suspicious domain
> extensions, missing HTTPS, and brand impersonation."

---

### 1:45 – 2:25 | Digital Arrest Scam Detection (PS6 highlight)
**(Show: Digital Arrest page → read the "how it works" panel → paste a
transcript like "This is CBI officer... stay on video call")**

> "This is our specialised module for Digital Arrest scams — the exact
> threat named in PS6. I'll paste a call transcript... and you can see it's
> instantly flagged as fraud with a dedicated alert: 'Hang up and call 1930,
> the Cyber Crime Helpline.' We built a safety override here — if two or
> more Digital Arrest patterns are detected, the system always flags it as
> fraud, because under-detecting this scam is simply too dangerous."

---

### 2:25 – 2:50 | UPI & QR Awareness
**(Show: UPI/QR page → check a UPI ID like "winner.lottery@upi")**

> "Fake UPI and QR scams are just as common. Here I'll check a suspicious
> UPI ID — the system flags the 'lottery' keyword instantly, and reminds
> users of the golden rule: scanning a QR code can only send money out,
> never bring money in."

---

### 2:50 – 3:15 | Reporting + Dashboard
**(Show: Report Scam form → submit → Dashboard with chart + history table)**

> "Users can report scams they encounter to help the community. And every
> scan is automatically logged to a personal Dashboard, with a live chart
> showing their Safe / Suspicious / Fraud breakdown over time."

---

### 3:15 – 3:40 | Admin Panel
**(Show: Log out → log in as admin → Admin Panel with stats + reports table)**

> "Finally, our Admin Panel gives platform administrators a bird's-eye view
> — total users, total scans, fraud detected, and every community-submitted
> scam report, which they can triage as Pending, Reviewed, or Resolved."

---

### 3:40 – 4:00 | Closing
**(Show: Home page again)**

> "ScamShield AI combines an explainable machine learning model with
> India-specific scam pattern detection to fight fraud, counterfeiting, and
> digital arrest scams — built end-to-end in Python, Flask, and
> scikit-learn. Thank you!"
