💡 AI-Generated Safety Tips

Practical, up-to-date guidance generated from patterns seen across thousands of real scam reports.

🔐

OTP & PIN Safety

Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or CVV with anyone — not even someone claiming to be from your bank, RBI, or the police. No legitimate entity ever needs it over a call.

📞

Unknown Calls & Videos Calls

If a caller creates panic and urgency ("arrest", "blocked", "seized"), pause. Real institutions communicate in writing and give you time to verify.

🔗

Links & Attachments

Don't click links in unexpected messages. Type the official website address yourself or use the verified app instead.

💳

Payments & Refunds

You never need to "pay" to receive a refund, prize, or lottery winning. Any request to pay first to unlock money is a scam.

👪

Involve Someone You Trust

Scammers isolate victims by saying "don't tell anyone." Genuine emergencies never require secrecy from family.

🧠

Slow Down

Scams rely on urgency to bypass your judgement. Take 5 minutes, verify independently, and the pressure usually reveals the scam.
