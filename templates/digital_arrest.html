🚨 Digital Arrest Scam Detection

India's fastest-growing scam: fraudsters impersonate police, CBI, customs or narcotics officers on a video call and threaten "digital arrest" to extort money. Learn to recognise it — and test suspicious messages below.

🎭 How the scam works

  1. You get a call/message claiming to be from police, CBI, customs, or narcotics department.
  2. They claim your Aadhar/PAN/parcel is linked to a crime (drugs, money laundering, etc).
  3. You're told to stay on a video call and not tell anyone — a fake "digital arrest".
  4. They pressure you to transfer money to a "safe" / "RBI verification" account.
  5. Once you pay, they disappear — this is 100% a scam. No real agency works this way.

✅ Golden rules to remember

  • No Indian law enforcement agency ever arrests you over a phone/video call.
  • No government agency asks you to transfer money to "verify" your identity.
  • You are always allowed to hang up, verify independently, and involve family.
  • Real police visits come with a written notice/warrant, not a surprise video call.
  • When in doubt: hang up and dial 1930 — the National Cyber Crime Helpline.
