📱 Fake UPI & QR Code Awareness

Understand how QR / UPI scams work and validate a UPI ID before you trust it.

⚠ The #1 myth to unlearn

Scanning a QR code or entering your UPI PIN can only send money out of your account — never receive it.

If someone asks you to scan a QR or enter your UPI PIN to "receive" a payment, refund, or cashback — it is always a scam.

🧾 Common Fake UPI/QR tricks

  • "Scan this QR to get your cashback / prize money."
  • Fake payment screenshots followed by "please refund the extra amount".
  • QR codes pasted over real ones at shops/parking (physical tampering).
  • Collect-request notifications disguised as "money received" alerts.
  • Urgent calls asking you to share UPI PIN "to reverse a wrong transaction".
